Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Chicken Tikka Masala, an iconic dish in Indian cuisine, beckons with its tantalizing blend of tender chicken and luscious, spiced tomato-based sauce. This culinary masterpiece begins with succulent pieces of chicken, marinated in a medley of yogurt and aromatic spices such as cumin, coriander, and turmeric, imparting depth and complexity to each bite. The marinated chicken is then grilled or roasted to perfection, infusing it with a tantalizing smoky flavor that serves as the perfect canvas for the forthcoming sauce. Meanwhile, a rich and creamy tomato-based sauce is prepared, bursting with the bold flavors of garlic, ginger, and chili peppers, simmered to perfection to achieve a harmonious balance of tanginess and heat. Once the sauce reaches its peak, the tender, marinated chicken is gently folded in, allowing it to soak up the luxurious flavors of the sauce while retaining its juicy texture.

The result is a symphony of flavors and textures – the succulent chicken marrying perfectly with the velvety sauce, creating a dish that is both comforting and indulgent. Chicken Tikka Masala isn't just a meal; it's a culinary journey that transports diners to the vibrant streets of India, where every bite is a celebration of spice, flavor, and tradition.

Ingredients

  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can chopped tomatoes
  • 4 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 2 cloves garlic, roughly chopped
  • 1 (1 inch) piece ginger, coarsely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons masala curry paste
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch pieces salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. Combine tomatoes, yogurt, garlic, and ginger in a blender and process until smooth.
  2. Heat oil in a large frying pan over medium heat. Add onion and fry until soft, 3 to 4 minutes, stirring constantly. Stir in curry paste and fry until fragrant, 1 minute more, stirring once or twice. Add the tomato mixture and chicken to the pan and mix together. Season with salt and pepper. Remove pan from heat.
  3. Mix water and flour together in a bowl. Stir into the chicken mixture in the frying pan. Return pan to the heat and bring to a boil, stirring constantly, about 5 minutes. Reduce heat to low, cover, and cook until thickened, about 15 minutes more. Sprinkle with cilantro and serve immediately.
